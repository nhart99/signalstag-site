Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-24

What we collect

When you join our “Follow for updates” list, we collect your email address and, if provided, your first name.

How we use your data

  • Send product updates, release notes, and announcements.
  • Manage double opt-in and unsubscribe preferences.

Where your data is stored

We use Brevo (Sendinblue) to manage our email list and send messages. Your data is processed according to Brevo’s policies and stored on their infrastructure. We do not sell your data.

Legal basis

We rely on your consent (opt-in) to send emails. You can withdraw consent anytime via the unsubscribe link in each email.

Retention

We retain your data while you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe or request deletion, we remove your data from our lists within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., log data for abuse prevention).

Third-party transfers

We do not share personal data beyond our email provider necessary to deliver emails. No advertising trackers or analytics are used on this site initially.

Cookies

This site is cookie-light. Embedded forms from our email provider may set strictly necessary cookies to operate double opt-in. If we add analytics later (e.g., Plausible), we will update this policy.

Your rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails or by emailing hello@signalstag.com.

Contact

Questions? support@signalstag.com
