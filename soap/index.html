WildSOAP

Offline SOAP note documentation for wilderness first responders. Built for remote environments where lives depend on accurate medical documentation.

Designed for WFR/WAFA certified responders, SAR teams, and wilderness guides operating in challenging conditions.

Get Updates Contact

Built for the Wilderness

  • Offline-first design: Works completely without internet connectivity - essential for remote wilderness locations.
  • Big-button interface: Large touch targets designed for use with gloves or in emergency situations.
  • Voice dictation: Native iOS/Android speech-to-text for hands-free documentation while treating patients.
  • Professional SOAP format: Structured Subjective, Objective, Assessment, and Plan sections following medical standards.
  • PDF export: Clean, professional documentation for handoff to EMS or medical facilities.
  • Auto-save functionality: Never lose critical patient information, even if the app crashes or device loses power.
  • GPS integration: Location tracking with privacy controls for accurate incident documentation.
  • Wide device compatibility: Works on older Android devices (API 23+) and iOS 13+ for maximum accessibility.

Who Uses WildSOAP

Wilderness First Responders

WFR and WAFA certified individuals working in remote locations where professional medical care is hours away.

Outdoor Educators
